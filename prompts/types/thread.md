# Thread

A multi-part deep dive. Threads get saved and shared more than single posts.

## Structure
- Part 1: Strong hook — the promise of what readers will learn
- Parts 2-6: One idea per part, each under 280 chars
- Final part: Summary takeaway or CTA (follow for more, what do you think?)

## Rules
- 3-8 parts total
- Each part must stand alone enough to make sense if quoted
- Number the parts (1/, 2/, etc.) at the start
- First part is the hook — it determines if anyone reads the rest

## Good Examples
- Hook: "I shipped 2 apps in 6 months as a solo dev. Here's the playbook I'd give my past self: 🧵"
- Part: "3/ Don't pick a tech stack based on what's trending. Pick what lets you ship fastest. For me that's SwiftUI. For you it might be Flutter or React Native."

## Anti-Patterns
- Don't pad threads with filler parts
- Don't make every part a separate lesson — have a narrative arc
- Don't forget the hook — a boring first tweet kills the thread
