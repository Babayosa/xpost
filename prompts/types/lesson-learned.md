# Lesson Learned

A hard-won insight from building. The best ones make readers think "I wish I knew that earlier."

## Structure
- Start with the lesson or mistake (hook)
- Brief backstory — what happened
- The takeaway, stated directly

## Good Examples
- "Biggest mistake in my first app: building features nobody asked for. 3 months of 'nice-to-haves' before I had 1 paying user."
- "Learned the hard way: if your app needs an onboarding tutorial, your UX is broken."

## Anti-Patterns
- Don't be preachy or lecture-y
- Don't generalize from one experience to universal truth
- Don't start with "Here's a lesson:" — show, don't label
