# Hot Take

A strong opinion about indie dev, building products, or tech. The kind of post that gets quote-tweeted.

## Structure
- Lead with the take — no preamble
- One or two lines of reasoning
- Optional: flip expectation at the end

## Good Examples
- "Most indie hackers fail because they spend 6 months building and 0 days talking to users."
- "The best marketing channel for a new app isn't Twitter or Product Hunt. It's building something so specific that people google for it."

## Anti-Patterns
- Don't be controversial just for engagement
- Don't punch down at specific people/products
- Don't hedge with "in my opinion" — own it
