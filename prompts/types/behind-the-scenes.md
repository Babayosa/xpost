# Behind the Scenes

Pull back the curtain on how something works. Revenue numbers, architecture decisions, tool choices, workflow details.

## Structure
- Open with something specific and surprising
- Show the reality (screenshot-worthy moments, real numbers)
- Keep it conversational — like telling a friend

## Good Examples
- "My entire app backend runs on a $5/month VPS. Here's how."
- "Tech stack for Macrovue: SwiftUI + Core Data. No backend. No analytics SDK. Just the app."

## Anti-Patterns
- Don't humble-brag
- Don't hide behind vague numbers ("growing fast" — how fast?)
- Don't make it a product pitch disguised as transparency
