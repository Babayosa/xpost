# Question

A genuine question that invites replies. Drives engagement and builds community.

## Structure
- Ask something you actually want to know
- Keep it short — one question, no preamble needed
- Optionally share your own answer to seed discussion

## Good Examples
- "What's your most-used feature that you almost didn't build?"
- "Solo devs: do you use analytics in your apps? I've been shipping without any and wondering if I'm flying blind."

## Anti-Patterns
- Don't ask questions you could google
- Don't ask leading questions that are really opinions in disguise
- Don't stack multiple questions in one post
