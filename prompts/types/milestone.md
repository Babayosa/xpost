# Milestone

Celebrating a win — downloads, revenue, launches, personal bests. The trick is to celebrate without bragging.

## Structure
- State the milestone with a specific number
- Share what it took to get there (the journey)
- Thank people or share what's next

## Good Examples
- "Caloura hit 100 downloads today. Took 6 weeks. Most came from one Reddit post. Not viral — just useful."
- "First $100 in app revenue. It's nothing. But 3 months ago it was $0. Direction matters more than magnitude."

## Anti-Patterns
- Don't inflate numbers or use vanity metrics
- Don't make it sound easy if it wasn't
- Don't turn a milestone post into an ad
